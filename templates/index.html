Your Next Steps
  • 1

    File Your LLC

    Open your LLC Filing Guide and follow the instructions to register with your state's filing portal. Save your approval documents once received.

  • 2

    Apply for Your EIN

    Go to irs.gov/ein, click "Apply Online Now," and use your EIN Answer Sheet — every question is pre-answered. Download your CP 575 confirmation letter when done.

  • 3

    Get Your Bank Letter Signed

    Bring your Banking Relationship Letter to your bank with a government-issued ID and your LLC approval documents. Ask your banker to complete and sign the letter.

  • 4

    Email Both Documents to Us

    Send photos or scans of your signed Bank Letter and EIN Confirmation Letter (CP 575) to documents@acmeatm.com — we'll take it from there.
